Baked Chicken

Juicy baked chicken suitable for IBD patients

Ingredients:

  • 2/3 pounds chicken, cut up,skin removed
  • 2 cups orange juice or low sodium chicken broth
  • 6 ounces tomato paste
  • 1/4 tablespoon vinegar
  • 2 teaspoons minced onion
  • 1/2 teaspoon celery seeds
  • 1/4 teaspoon dry mustard
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/8 teapoon groung cinnamon
  • 1/8 teaspoon ground cloves

Directions:

  1. Place chicken lighty grased baking dish
  2. Combine reaming ingredients in saucepan; bring it to boil
  3. Reduce heat and simmer 5 minutes, Pour over chicken
  4. Bake, uncovered at 350 for 1 hour or until done, basting frequently
