Oats Banana smoothie

Healthy oats smoothie made with ripe bananas

Ingredients:

  • 2 ice cubes
  • 2 small ripe bananas
  • 1/3 cup unsweetened soy or almond milk, or use half soy or almond milk
    and half plain nonfat or lowfat yogurt
  • 2 tablespoon cooked steelcut oats

Directions:

  1. Place ice cubes into blender and blend to crush
  2. Add the remaining ingredients; blend until smooth.
